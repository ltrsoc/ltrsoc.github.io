About LTR

Who we are and what we stand for

LTR is an independent organization founded in 2022. We develop software, conduct research, and pursue projects that we consider meaningful. Our work spans utilities, distributed systems, and experimental tools.

We operate without external oversight and answer to no one but ourselves. The organization is small by design — quality and intent take precedence over scale.
