About LTR

Who we are and what we stand for

LTR is an independent organization founded in 2022. It was created purely by fate. A few random seconds and chance encounters decided everything. If not for those moments, LTR would never have existed at all. We have no external funding, no investors, and no oversight. We answer only to ourselves.

We develop software, conduct research, and make projects that we consider meaningful. Our work spans utilities, distributed systems, experimental tools and projects where we integrate AI.

We operate without external oversight and answer to no one but ourselves. The organization is small because we are not recognizable yet.
